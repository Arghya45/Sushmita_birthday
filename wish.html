Wishes for the Birthday Girl

Dear Jashmine

The only reason I feel I am a lucky man is that I got to meet you. You have changed my life for the better, made me a happier and better man, and all it took was one smile. That smile will live in my dreams forever, even though every moment I spend with you feels like a dream, so I suppose, your smile will always be what I seek out in my life. Happy birthday, I love you! My dear wife, may your birthday be as happy as you have made me. Happy birthday!
